Stove-top Jasmine Rice Recipe

Home

Description

Plain jasmine rice cooked on a stove-top is a staple food. It serves as a foundation of many great dishes and is pretty good all on it's own!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup jasmine rice, dry

Directions

  1. Fill small sauce pan with 2 cups of water and bring to a boil.
  2. Once water is boiling, add 1 cup of rice to the water. Stir rice to prevent clumping.
  3. Place cover on the saucepan and reduce the heat to low. Cook for 12 minutes and remove from heat.
